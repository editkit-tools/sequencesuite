Release notes

SequenceSuite 1.7.0

A tidier sidebar you can clear in one move, and markers that read the same whichever NLE they came from.

Keeping your sidebar under control

  • Close All Files clears the sidebar in one move, from the Sequence menu or the Open Files header. Long sessions no longer mean closing files one at a time.
  • Close Other Files keeps the sequence, file or multi-track cut you right-clicked and closes the rest.
  • Remove Missing Files prunes anything that no longer resolves on disk. Drives that are simply unmounted are left alone, so an offline shoot drive stays in your list until you decide otherwise.

Markers and posters

  • One marker vocabulary across every NLE. Markers now read as Standard, Incomplete to-do, Completed to-do or Chapter whether they arrived from Premiere, Resolve, Final Cut, an EDL or an OTIO.
  • Marker reports carry a Visible column, so hidden markers stay visible to you in the report even when they are not in the timeline.
  • Poster frames at any height from 40px to 1280px, chosen from a menu in Settings, for thumbnails big enough to actually read.

Around the suite

  • Hover any shortened name in the sidebar and the full name appears, including paths in the Inspector, Settings and the Source Access and Sibling Results sheets.
  • Hovering a multi-track cut lists the EDLs inside it, in track order.
  • Plenty of small fixes and refinements throughout.
