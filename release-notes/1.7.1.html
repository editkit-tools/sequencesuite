Release notes

SequenceSuite 1.7.1

Split edits now identify every leg, so nothing in your Sheet reads as a mystery clip.

Split edits, fully named

  • Both legs of a split carry the source clip name. When an L-cut or J-cut splits a clip's sound and picture across the timeline, every row is named, not just the one where the picture lands.
  • A split archive or stock shot reads as itself on every leg, so researchers and production tracking can see at a glance what a clip is and where it came from.

Around the suite

  • The Open Files header holds steady as you move across it.
  • Small fixes and refinements throughout.
