Release notes

SequenceSuite 1.6.0

Gather, rebuilt around one clear plan - with copies you can vouch for, and a lot less waiting.

Your Gather Plan

  • One plan, one name. Your Gather Plan holds the files you tick, Planned Files lists exactly those and nothing else, and a Plan / This Sequence switch moves between the two.
  • Gather This Sequence copies just the sequence in front of you and leaves the plan you built last week as you left it.
  • Your plan is remembered between launches.
  • Plan offline media. Tick files while the drive is still on the shelf, mount it, and run the copy.

Copying with confidence

  • Live progress while copies are verified, with Pause and Stop available at any point in the run.
  • Copy verification confirms every file against the physical media, and anything it cannot vouch for is reported as an issue.
  • Choose your destination with one click, and jump to it in Finder any time - including straight from the completion screen.
  • One clean text report per copy. Want a spreadsheet-friendly version too? Switch it on in Settings.

Around the suite

  • Moving between the plan and a sequence is instant, with Rescan when you want a fresh look at your drives.
  • Large sessions come back more quickly at launch.
  • Open an Avid AAF in Sheet mode and a single click takes you to Gather, where AAFs live.
  • A friendlier welcome window, and the tour can be run again whenever you like.
  • Plenty of small fixes and refinements throughout.
