Release notes

SequenceSuite 1.2.3

Sharper duplicate detection for graphics and titles, with new media-type filters across Dupe and Sheet.

Finding duplicates

  • Graphics and titles are told apart by name - distinct lower thirds, Astons and generators each stand as their own clip, across Premiere, FCPXML, Resolve and Avid EDL exports.
  • Avid titles are recognised - a title from an Avid EDL is labelled as a graphic.

Filtering by media type

  • Audio, Video, Stills and Generated buckets in both Dupe and Sheet, with a badge on every row so you can see at a glance what each clip is.
  • Generated graphics are hidden by default in Dupe so they stay out of your way; stills are shown. Toggle any bucket on or off as you work.
