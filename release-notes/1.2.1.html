Release notes

SequenceSuite 1.2.1

A small polish release for the keyboard handling and selection totals introduced in 1.2.0.

Refinements

  • Selection totals on mixed-rate timelines — the running total for a selection adds up at the sequence's frame rate, so the figure matches your timeline.
  • Sharper arrow-key handling — a plain arrow key moves the highlighted row; add Shift to stretch the selection.
  • Your place is kept — the selected row stays selected while you bring in more EDLs.
